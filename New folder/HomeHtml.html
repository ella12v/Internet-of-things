The Internet Of Things And Security!

The internet of things, or IoT, is a framework of interrelated computing gadgets, mechanical and
computerized machines, objects, creatures or individuals that are given unique identifiers (UIDs)
and the capacity to exchange data over a network without requiring human-to-human or
human-to-computer interaction.
